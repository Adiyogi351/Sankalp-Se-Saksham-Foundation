About Us
Sankalp Se Saksham Foundation is a registered NGO with a unique mission to educate society, not just with academics, but with moral values and a deep connection to our culture.

We are committed to eradicating crime, corruption, and negativity by nurturing better human beings. Through our efforts, we help India move towards becoming a proud Vishwaguru.
